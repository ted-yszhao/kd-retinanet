\chapter{Future Plan}
Clearly there will be a significant amount of work still to complete beyond your interim report. It is essential at the interim stage that you are able to realise what these tasks are that you need to complete, what they will involve and what results they will show beyond what you have achieved so far. You need to give the confidence to your examiners that you have understood what you are doing so far, that you know what you are going to do next to develop those results and be sure you are going to meet your objectives by the end of the project. You will find that it is useful to refer to specific tasks in the Gantt chart in appendix 1 to identify clear milestones that you will reach.
